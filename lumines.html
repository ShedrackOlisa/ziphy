Gameplay Overview

In Lumines, players must arrange falling blocks of various colors and patterns to form 2x2 squares called "lumines," which are then cleared from the board. The twist lies in the game's synchronized music and timeline, where the board's horizontal line sweeps across the screen in time with the beat of the background music. This adds an extra layer of challenge and strategy, as players must time their moves to coincide with the music's rhythm to create larger combos and clear more blocks. As the game progresses, the pace quickens, presenting players with increasingly challenging patterns and speeds to keep them engaged and on their toes.
