Gameplay Overview

In Burnout Legends, players compete in fast-paced races against AI-controlled opponents or other players in various game modes. The game emphasizes aggressive driving and rewards players for taking risks, such as driving in oncoming traffic, narrowly avoiding collisions, and executing near-misses to build up their boost meter. Boost can be used to increase speed and perform special maneuvers, such as drifts and takedowns, which are essential for winning races and earning points.
